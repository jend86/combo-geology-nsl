CHAPTER IV. INSTRUMENTAL FORMATIONS

Instrumental formations play an important role in the geological structure of formations and the development of tension machines. Their complexes are composed of middle and upper strata; diorites, granodiorites, granites and their subsequent intrusions. In the increased quantity widespread instrumental rocks of basic and alkaline composition.

The majority of instrumental rocks, identified in the studied territory, have a single-stage structure and are rarely decorated by rocks of different instrumental complexes.
