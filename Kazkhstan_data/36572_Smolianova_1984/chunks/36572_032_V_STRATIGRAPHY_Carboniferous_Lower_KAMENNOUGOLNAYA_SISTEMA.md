KAMENNOUGOLNAYA SISTEMA

Deposits of the coal-bearing system are widely distributed within the studied territory. Their boundaries are generally confined to the Teniz Depression and to areas of large anticlinical structures of the internal parts of the depression. In the region under study, work has been carried out on all departments of the coal-bearing system.
