CLASTIC ROCKS.

Rocks of these series and coarse-fine grain size show predominantly normal-marine structure. The quantity of terrigenous minerals does not exceed 40-60%, with dominant pyroxenes. The quantity of spar is reduced to complete disappearance. With increasing rock maturity, there are gradual transitions.

Second instrumental suite

Rocks of the second instrumental suite in the work areas of the Dzhezkazgan, Sarychokan and Koktosai deposits. The main petrographic variability of the formations of this suite on the examined site consists of biotite-biotite and biotite-amphibole granite. Greater mineralogical variability, represented usually by granodiorites, is encountered rarely.
