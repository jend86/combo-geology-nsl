2. Geological Research
(pages 23 sheets 1,2,c)

The first geological investigations in the fields of scientific territorial planning were conducted in sorochinsk-pestovsk regions. At that time, accumulated 1:1000000-1:500000 geological maps (N-9, P.I. Symonov; N-8 Butenko B.I.; N-5 Kudin U.I.; K-30, Ivanov S.V.; N-12 Artsev B.I.; N-14, K-30 Seryozov V.I.).

As a result of completed paleontological field work, a large collection of Tenseian fauna was discovered; a large lower zone was identified, which is of interest for understanding the structure; extensive ore mineralization was revealed (N-30, Seryozov A.I.).

In subsequent years of work, research was conducted, mainly on the character of the paleontological associations (N-14, Shiker D.I.).

In the 50-60s years at Tenseian field, large-scale work was conducted at 1:200000-1:100000 scale with the application of complete geological methods.

The tasks of these works were included in territorial zoning of the region, identification of local structures, compilation of synthetic structure-geological maps of regional function.

A number of these works were carried out with assistance.

For this role, reconnaissance aerogeological work, MG SSALT, MT SUT, SGT fully covered the area of geological study of aeronautical, grain-structural composition at 1:200000-1:100000 scale.

As a result of the completed works, a map was compiled showing force effects and reduction of Bouguer, partial ΔT, ΔZ, tent-theoretical anomalies. Identified drilling anomalies corresponded to observations.

In the late 50s, early 60s, the methodology of geological work, which expanded the capability of apparatus, was perfected.
