REFERENCE

on work completed on Western Kazakhstan from January-April 1982
and December 1981.

Western Kazakhstan Party approved in December 1981 from April 1982, was work on useful minerals obtained in 1981 on sheets L-42-I, VI, VII and continued mineral work on sheets L-42-19, U, L-42-XIII, XXIV, XXV.

As a result of work completed:

1. Preliminary geological maps of sheets M-43-II, VI, VIII at 1:200000 scale.

2. Maps of geological material of sheets L-42-I, VII, VIII at 1:200000 scale.

3. Preliminary geological maps based on detailed survey of sheets L-42-XXIII, XXIV, XXV, L-42-IV, J at 1:100000 on photomaps.

4. Preliminary maps of useful mineral deposits of sheets L-42-XIV, U, VI, VII, VIII, IX, I, II at 1:200000 scale.

5. Maps of geological material of sheets L-42-XXIII, XXIV, XXV at 1:100000 scale.

6. Section "Geological Scientific Features" in the report.

7. Sections: "Paleozoic deposits" and "Mesozoic deposits" in the chapter "Stratigraphy."

8. Geological column on revealed results of mineral prospecting and development.

The Commission recommends the following further work:

1. Pay special attention to the study of the Devonian deposits in the Ustyurt-Mangystau and Mangystau areas on the Aral Sea.

2. Carry out verification of field survey results taking into account structural conditions of the Cis-Caspian geo-prospecting expedition within the Vladimirova anticline and Karabogusu uplift.

3. When describing ore deposits, provide information on main western deposits; determination of remaining Vladimirova deposits of their position and [illegible].

CONCLUDING REMARKS.

In connection with preliminary data on the occurrence of oil and gas in the Caspian Depression in the Kazakhstan region, based on the results of determining microfauna and flora, paleontological collections were made from organic residues in the reference section on the north coast of the Caspian Depression.

Materials are presented in liquid volume, compiled methodologically correctly.

Geological work was completed in a workmanlike manner, materials give grounds for assessing it as "good".
