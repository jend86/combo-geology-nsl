CASE: report B.V. Smyshlyaeva on the status of large-scale
exploration of petroleum resources 1:200000 in the southern part of Mangystau
region. Discussed and considered report B.V. Smyshlyaeva of NGO
"Centrgazgeologiya" branch, which noted that regional work on scale 1:200000
in the Mangystau region is conducted in accordance with approved programs.

In connection with the fact that the period of work was significant
(1970-1984), and on the territory of Mangystau region during the current time a ban has been imposed on large-scale geological mapping at scale 1:50000, general reasons, regional separate prospecting work, clarification of stratigraphic boundaries, drilling of parametric holes, there was necessity to concentrate the volume of geological-prospecting work in the current project West-Caspian PSP:

1. In the project it was necessary to conduct drilling of a parametric borehole
on the west-Mangystau army of the Amudarya cupola of volume 1550 m.v.
At the current time on this site exploratory work had proceeded,
which allowed to study the section of superimposed formations. In addition
on this site the Amudarya cupola required a parametric survey of petroleum-
bearing formations, on which research of the kernel was conducted.
