The thickness of the Lower Kayraktin substage is 155 m. Two members can be identified in the section: the lower one — argillaceous-terrigenous-carbonate, with abundant interbeds of limestones saturated with ostracod fauna, the limestones are sometimes bituminous with a hydrogen sulfide odor; the upper one — predominantly sandy, with numerous plant remains. Due to poor surface exposure, the members are not mapped.
