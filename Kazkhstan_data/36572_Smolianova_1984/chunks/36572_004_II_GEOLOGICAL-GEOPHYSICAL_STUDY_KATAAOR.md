The office of "Spetsgeo izshka", the Step Expedition, KaziT,
and Skge cover the territory of the geological study with a geographic
survey. Geological and structural geochemical maps are being compiled,
intrusive intrusions and tectonic disturbances have been mapped;
promising areas for copper have been identified.

In accordance with the decision of the section of solid minerals of the
Scientific and Technical Council of the Kazakh SSR MG "On the state and ways to
increase the efficiency of mining operations for copper in Central Kazakhstan.
Teniz region"... the further expansion of the base of the Dzhezkazgan combine
of Dalian will be carried out due to the localization of geological prospects
of neighboring areas. This is, first of all, the Teniz depression, the
state of study of which did not allow for a quantitative assessment of its
projected reserves.

In this regard, in the 70s and 80s, the Shadow basin was systematically
studied by the expeditions of the Central Committee of the Russian Academy of Sciences,
the SKI complex of geological works - to a considerable extent by seismic exploration in order
to search for structures promising for copper.

As a result of the work carried out, clear reflecting horizons
have been seen, stratigraphically linked from the bottom of the winter
turn to the bottom of the Vladimirovskaya formation, box-perspective,
copper-perspective sites have been seen, ideas about the
structural control of copper mining and its placement on the rails
of brachianticline deposits have been confirmed.

More detailed information about the geological surveys in
the vicinity of the territorium under study, including the 1:50,000
geographic survey work, is reflected in the geographic survey
catalog (Table 5).
