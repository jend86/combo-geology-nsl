**Kumansai Suite (O₂km)**

The location of the Kumansai suite distribution on the right bank of the Kum River in the near-floodplain organization. According to scientific data, it is found in the Saryesik pedagogical organization; in the area of the village of Dmitryevka, and to the east of r. Aksodor (N-42-sh).
