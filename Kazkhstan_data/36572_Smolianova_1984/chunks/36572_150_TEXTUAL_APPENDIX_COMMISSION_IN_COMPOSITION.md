Commission Recommends:

1. Given that, in the area of the Aksubensky field (map H-42-54) a large amount of oil was found by side organizations, the documents of which are in the files, transfers permitted in the area and to the south-east from 09.Lenin.

2. In further work, to determine the special importance of studying the upper paleozoic, for which purpose: to conduct a survey of structures in the area of maps H-42-54, H-42-56.

3. Given that the stratigraphic section encountered in the area is in a wide regional area and is not accessible for troubleshooting, conduct a survey of the upper paleozoic sections of the Amphibian depression with the aim of identifying here parastratigraphic markers of this section.

4. Construct structural-tectonic profile using materials from available geological work, including - seismic (where it is available).

5. Request test drilling from the younger strata of the named depression to determine absolute age.
