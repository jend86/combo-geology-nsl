NATURAL COMPOSITION OF BREEDS OF THE DRABULSKOYE COMPLEX

AMPHIBOLITE DIORITE - granite forms the basis of the breed type of the drabulskoye complex in the opposition district. Macroscopically this species, clearly porphyra, practically do not contain homogeneous minerals. Structure of breeds srednezernitsa, sredne-samozernitsa, rarely porphyritic. Microstructure porphyritic, granular.

The main breed-distinguishing minerals are quartz, feldspar (polite shale and plagioclase). Quantitatively mineral composition of breed common limestone breeds according to one measurement (in 4524/1) as follows: quartz - 42.5%, feldspat - 39.5%, plagioclase - 27.5%. More detailed investigations in breeds of Karatashskoye placement show that content of minerals may vary in quite wide limits (in this respect): 34-47%; 21-40%; 25-57% (Grachiev, 1970).

Quartz monocrystalline, surrounding grain size from 0.6 to 4 mm, usually combined in large aggregates.

Feldspar polite shale - rarely monocrystalline, forms widely faceted crystal faces of small size (to 0.7-1 cm in porphyritic distributions). Usually size does not exceed 1 mm.

Porphyritic, partly lens-like and vein-like, often distributed in places under which breeds granulate between objects.

Idiomorph - grain size from 0.4 to 4 mm (usually 1-1.5mm). Idiomorphic tabular are crystal. Size polymetamorphic propensity (albite-type), cleavage which give bread-like (approximately 25), in control clean crystal coaxing.

Magmatic education of Silurian-Riphean age

During the Silurian-Riphean period of magmatic activity, in the limits of the studied territory, two magmatic complexes of different magnetic composition were formed, distinguished by composition of breed of their genesis: 1) granite-plutonic complex of pale effusions and intrusives, and 2) volcanic complex of granites.
