As is known, the largest concentration of copper mineralization of strata-bound type in Kazakhstan was the Dzhezkazgan deposit, which is the only one for deposits of the investigated type of copper mineralization in Kazakhstan, and also for manifestations of the investigated region.

The main factors controlling all mineralization on the Dzhezkazgan field location are:

1. Stratigraphic control of mineralization (middle and upper carboniferous).
