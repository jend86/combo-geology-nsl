**Copper occurrence Vladimirovskoye** | C₃tr

Thirteen lens-shaped ore bodies have been identified. Length of ore bodies 0.3–3.0 m. Thickness 0.2–2.5 m. Copper content in groove samples by spectral analysis 0.2–5.0%. Copper content in primary ores reaches 0.42% (borehole B P–204).

Discovered by I.A. Eliginin and V.M. Popov in 1929. Investigated in 1940. (Bogomozov, 1940.) Further studied by I.B. Dmitrovsky (1969), B. Seidullin (1976) and V.A. Pashadsky (1980). Drilling, core sampling, spectral analysis. **Unpromising.**
