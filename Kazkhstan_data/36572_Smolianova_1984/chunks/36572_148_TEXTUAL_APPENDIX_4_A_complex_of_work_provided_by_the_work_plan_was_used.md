4. A complex of work provided by the work plan was used.

5. Timeline for completion of the stage of work:
Start – May 1979, factually – May 1979.
Completion – May 1980, factually – May 1980.

6. The following main results were obtained:

a) Composite field geological maps at scale 1:200000 were compiled based on published specialized research as well as in stratigraphic distribution of ore-bearing formations and in structure.

b) In the study of ore-bearing formations, significant attention was paid to searches for ore-bearing zones in the archelinian and svecofennian stratigraphic intervals.

Detailed studies of individual sections of the metamorphic composition of formations and the nature of changes in facies in space and time.

7. Remarks concerning the technical deficiencies:

a) On our maps on geological maps, isolated sections, pyramided and depicted in geomorphological isolation of the region.

b) No analysis was conducted regarding the distribution of mineralization on a new structural basis.

8. Recommendations:

A) Compile schemes of possibilities and facies for individual series, packs, formations.

B) Using geological materials, conduct analysis of the dependence of the structural plan of the Berkakhalosovsky section on its foundation.
