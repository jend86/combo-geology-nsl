In the area of the reported structural complex, besides the basic Pliocene suite, numerous finds of mollusks and ostracods have been determined, distributed in individual breeds and sporadic complexes.

From the Peri-Caspian suite, a large quantity of ostracods was identified, the determinations of which were made by S.V. Namutova. The results of the investigations are given in Table 29, 29a, compiled in stratigraphic-zonal order. The visible association of ostracods in all zones is uniform, both in composition and distribution. For the endemic complex, an insignificant quantity (17-30%) of submersed species and 4-15% Ponto-Caspian species are noted. The quantity of Ponto-Caspian species increases in the Upper Cretaceous section, and in the Eocene-Oligocene they are practically absent. The distribution of ostracods by area is uneven. Locations of ostracods in the bulk do not have small-sized forms (Amnilacea multa, Auleskaya multa), and in individual sections they are not present (Sendia multa, Vladimiroskaya and Tretyankovskaya multa).

In the complex discussed here, the following species were noted:
Darwinula gabcavi Bolous., D.malachovensis Bolous., D.stellochocovella Bolous., D.stellmachovella Bolous., D.ordinata De-lous., D.fidelis Kaph., D.fractiformis Bolous., D.nasaliformis Bolous., D.kasachstanica Bolous.

In the upper-seminal-gully species not previously encountered in the complex are noted: Darwinula fortschenkoi Busch., D.timida Busch., D.clara Rosch., D.nova Busch.
