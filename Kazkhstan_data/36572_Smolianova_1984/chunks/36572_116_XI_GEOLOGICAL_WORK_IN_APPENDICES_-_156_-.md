1.1. Instrumentation methods

Geological surveys were carried out using stations AZK-1500 and SKD-69 with pneumatic protection. Registration of diamagnetism was performed using gradient-sonde AI, 210, I.I. The results obtained were related to the dependence on electrical resistance of hot coal and existing conditions. In connection with large volume of work on porosity determination, bore holes were sampled in narrow intervals, corresponding to 10 to 100 cm and 1 cm.

A total diamagnetism was performed using standard installation with special electrode, in spacing 12.5-25 /cm, with speed no more than 500 m/h.

For quality assessment and accuracy of measurements at this site, repeated measurements were performed in the interval no less than 10³ of the following interval. Layout calculations of measurement error were not exceeded 10%.
