CONCLUSION

on Stage 01 "Pre-Field Office Work" for the geological assignment of the West-Teniz Party, approved by the Chief Engineer of the CKTGU on 3 November 1977

Commission composed of:

ANTONIKA R.M. – Commission Chairman, Acting Chief Geologist of CGGЕ

VURMAN V.I. – Chief Geophysicist
SMOLYANINOVA G.N. – Head of the Geophysical Department
MINIKINA E.A. – Acting Head of PZO
GULYAEVA A.A. – Geodesist of CGGЕ

Having reviewed the results of the pre-field office work and the information received (project, estimate, photogeological maps at scale 1:100,000, materials from previous investigations and core analysis of boreholes), the Commission accepts the above-mentioned stage of work with a rating of "excellent" for submission for payment at the estimated cost of actually completed work volumes in the amount of **28,944 rubles**.

The quality assessment and payment amount were determined based on the following criteria:

**1.** The geological assignment for the stage has been completed.

**2.** All submitted materials, both in volume and quality, correspond to the substance of the assigned task.

**3.** No deviations from the accepted methodology.

**4.** Utilization of the work complex provided for in the stage plan.

**5. Stage completion schedule:**

Start — January 1978, actual — January 1978
Completion — May 1979, actual — April 1979

**6. The following principal results were obtained:**

a) Detailed photogeological maps meeting current requirements have been compiled.

b) A catalogue of geological-geophysical study coverage has been compiled for the area of geological follow-up survey.

**The Commission recommends:**

1. Prior to departure for field work, to decrypt [interpret] space aerial photographs.

2. To plot geophysical anomalies and directions of seismic profiles onto geological maps.

3. To plot copper occurrences onto geological maps and attempt to identify the patterns of their distribution.
