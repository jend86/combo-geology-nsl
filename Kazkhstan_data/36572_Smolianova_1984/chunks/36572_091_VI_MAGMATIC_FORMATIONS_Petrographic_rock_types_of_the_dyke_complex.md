A similar dike complex is also characteristic of the Obalykol massif, although bodies composed of intermediate-composition rocks have not been noted there. The petrographic characteristics of the rocks of the dike complex are summarized in Table No. 56, and the results of two silicate analyses of samples collected by us from the dike bodies of the Obalykol massif are presented in Table No. 57.

The age of the dike complex is established as Early Devonian on the grounds that they cut across the granitoids of the Borovsky complex. Since similar dikes are unknown among the volcanogenic formations of the Lower–Middle Devonian, it may be assumed that their emplacement took place within a narrow time interval preceding the period of accumulation of these volcanics.
