Based on the obtained scheme of tectonic structure of the Ustyurt anticlinorium, an assumption was made about the coincidence of block structure of the foundation with structures of the upper Paleozoic.

At the final stage, with the help of various methods of quantitative interpretation of gravitational and magnetic anomalies, a probable assessment was made of the geometric parameters of the objects studied (depth of occurrence of blood, angles of contact dip).

Quantitative interpretation of geophysical materials proceeded in stages:

- determination of geometric and physical parameters of the studied geological objects;

- indication of the data obtained concerning geological structure locations.

When interpreting magnetic anomalies, analytical methods and the method of analogues were used.

Qualitative interpretation of gravitational anomalies was performed with the assistance of a team of specialists from VSEGEI, Sieverdnikov L.B.

Selection of sections according to interpretation profiles (see. ) was carried out according to paleotectonic maps N.V. Tampike.

The following assumptions were made in the calculations:

- all geological bodies were considered as two-dimensional;

- density of stratigraphic strata were selected as measured according to measurements;

Results of quantitative interpretation were used in carrying out geological sections. [illegible] sections according to interpretation profiles indicated data about geological discoveries and drilling.

All data from quantitative interpretation were plotted on the scheme of the structure of the Paleozoic foundation (see. ).

CHAPTER V. STRATIGRAPHY

In the construction of the scientific territory, located within the western part of the Tenizsian depression, northern edge of the Sarysu-Teniz uplift, eastern edge of the Ustyurt mass, there participate variously aged, distributed and metamorphic rocks, relating by age to the Proterozoic, Paleozoic and Cainozoic groups.
