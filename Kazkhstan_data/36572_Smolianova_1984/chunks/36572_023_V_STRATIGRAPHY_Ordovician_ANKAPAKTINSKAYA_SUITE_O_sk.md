**Ordovician Deposits**

Karatauan suite (O₃ kmg) and Myuassamptoran suite (O₃ kb)

Rocks belonging to these deposits, which are part of the upper part of the Ordovician system, are located on the scientific territory in an isolated location, on the slope of the Saryshagan Range. The described breeds suggest significant weakness of the northern-southwestern projection, similar to the pattern of major structural disturbances. The Karatauan suite in the given section consists of dolomitic-limestone-sedimentary and sedimentary rocks of Fusulinella, Fusulinortis, Liperitina and Parafusulina genera, with greenish, blue-green and green coloration. In the upper part of the section, rocks are represented by sandstone layers. The thickness of the suite is 50m.
