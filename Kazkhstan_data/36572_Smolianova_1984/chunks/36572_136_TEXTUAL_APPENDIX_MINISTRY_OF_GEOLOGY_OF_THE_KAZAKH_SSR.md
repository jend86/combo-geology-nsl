# CERTIFICATE

on the results of the field survey work on the Zapadno-Toleiskoye PSI area for February–April 1982 and December 1981.

The field party was assigned a survey period from December 1981 to April 1982. The task involved processing field materials received in 1981, deciphering aerial photographs for sheets M-43-II, VI, VII, II and performing field work on sheets M-43-XIV, Y; M-43-XXIX, XXXIV, XXXV.

As a result of the work, the following was compiled:

1. Preliminary geological survey maps for sheets M-43-II, VI, VII, II at scale 1:200,000.

2. Maps of actual material for sheets M-43-II, VI, VII, II at scale 1:200,000.

3. Preliminary geological survey maps based on the results of deciphering sheets M-43-XXXIII, XXXIV, XXXV, M-43-IV, V at scale 1:100,000 on photoplans.

4. Preliminary mineral occurrence survey maps for sheets M-43-XIV, XV, VI, VII, IX, II, III at scale 1:200,000.

5. Maps of actual material for sheets M-43-XXXIII, XXXIV, XXXV at scale 1:100,000.

6. Section "Geological Survey" for the report.

7. Sections: "Cenozoic Deposits" and "Lower Paleozoic Deposits" for the chapter "Stratigraphy."

8. Geological columns from boreholes with the results of drilling operations and sampling.

**REPORT**
on the completion of office (камеральных) works for the Atbasarskaya
field survey party (ПСП) for November 1980 – May 1981

The party's assignment for the office period from November 1980 to May 1981 was the completion of works on the project of the Ladyzhenskaya field survey party with defense of the report at the Scientific-Technical Council (NTS) in 1980, processing of field materials for the Zapadno-Tenizskaya field survey party on sheets М-42-IX (western half), VII (eastern half), XIV, XV, compilation of preliminary geological maps based on pre-field interpretation, with reference to archival literature for the western half of sheet М-42-VII, the eastern half of sheet М-42-VII, sheets М-42-II, III (partially).

As a result of the work performed:

1. The report on the Ladyzhenskaya field survey party was compiled, defended at the NTS of the association, and submitted to the archives.

2. Map layouts at scale 1:200,000 were compiled for sheets М-42-IX (western half), VII (eastern half), XIV, XV.

3. Cadastres of occurrences for the same area and preliminary maps of mineral resources were compiled.

4. Based on the results of pre-field interpretation, preliminary maps were compiled for the areas of sheets М-42-VII (western half), М-42-VII (eastern half), М-42-II, III.

5. Tracings and extracts from works of previous years were made.

6. Composite cross-sections were compiled and their correlation was carried out for the western part of the Teniz depression.

The commission recommends for further work:

1. To refine the drafting of faults and classify them by morphology and significance on map layouts at scale 1:200,000.

2. On the mineral resource maps, to overlay geochemical halos based on materials from lithogeochemical surveys of previous years.

3. Drill-hole locations for 1981 to be confirmed with the North Kazakhstan Production Association.

The submitted materials are methodologically correct. Materials are submitted in the required volume. The geological assignment has been completed satisfactorily.
