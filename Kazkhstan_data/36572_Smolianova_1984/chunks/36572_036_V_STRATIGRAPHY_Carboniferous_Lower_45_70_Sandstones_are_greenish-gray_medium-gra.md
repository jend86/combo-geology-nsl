The following lithological description of the rocks of the Kipshaks formation cannot be considered complete and exhaustive, since it covers only sections in the southern part of the Teniz depression. This is explained by the absence of petrographic material from the northern sections by the authors of the report.

The terrigenous rocks of the Kipshak formation in the south of the depression are represented by sandstones, siltstones, and mudstones, as well as (in sharply subordinate amounts) infraformational gravelites.
