REPORT
on the completion of geological work on automobile exploration
of the USSR for November 1980 – May 1981

During the reporting period from November 1980 to May 1981, work was completed on the Ladheshevsk project of automobile exploration of the USSR in 1980. The works included: materials from the Western-Tien-Shan USSR sections M-42-IX (western foothills), VIII (eastern foothills), XIV, XV; the remainder of preliminary geological maps on the scale 1:50,000 for the southern part of sheet M-42-V, eastern part of sheet M-42-VI, sheets M-42-I, (partially).

As a result of the preceding work:

1. Compiled, corrected and bound in one report the Ladheshevsk USSR sections.

2. Compiled a set of maps of mountains 1:250000 on sheets M-42-IX (western foothills), VIII (eastern foothills), XIV, XV.

3. Compiled cadastral records for the same sections and preliminary maps of useful minerals.

4. According to the results of previous field survey work, compiled preliminary maps on sheets M-42-VIII (western foothills), M-42-VII (eastern foothills), M-42-I, II.

5. Extracts and annotations from work of previous years were made.

6. Disputes were settled and their resolution carried out in the western part of Tien-Shan.

Commission recommends for further work:

1. Clarify the distribution of sections and subdivide them by methodology and scale on maps of mountains 1:50000.

2. On the maps of useful minerals, apply geological medium categories according to lithogeochemical surveys of previous years.

3. Location of establishment of sections in 1981 should take into account North-Kazakhstan regional consolidation conducted.

Preliminary materials are returned by separate mail. Materials are provided in autumn. Geological work completed at quality level.
