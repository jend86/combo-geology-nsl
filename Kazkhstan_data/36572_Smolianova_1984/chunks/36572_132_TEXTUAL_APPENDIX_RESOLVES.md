**HEARD:** Report by E.M. Smolyaninova on the status of group detailed geological survey at scale 1:200,000 in the western part of the Teniz Depression. Having heard and discussed the report by E.M. Smolyaninova, the STC of the Association notes that the regional survey work at scale 1:200,000 in the Teniz Depression is being conducted methodologically correctly and in accordance with the approved project.

In view of the fact that the timeframe for the work is considerable (1978–1984), and that at present, simultaneously alongside this work in the territory of the Teniz Depression, group geological mapping at scale 1:50,000, general prospecting, regional seismic exploration, and reference parametric drilling are being conducted, the need has arisen to adjust the scope of geological-exploration work within the active project of the West-Teniz FSP:

**I.** The project provided for the drilling of a buried cross-section along the southwestern limb of the Aksuy dome with a volume of 1,550 linear meters. To date, a borehole profile has been drilled along this line allowing the study of the upper Paleozoic sequence. In addition, on the southern limb of the dome, reference-parametric borehole No. 019 has been drilled, with core recovery completed.

2. It is advisable to replace the profile of boreholes of the covered cross-section on sheet M-42-VII with documentation from reference-parametric boreholes I4B, I4B, drilled by the Turgai GRE, and to replace the projected borehole on the Kokchetav uplift with documentation from reference-parametric boreholes OI5, II-I5, OI5 A, OI7 of the Dzhezkazgan GRE. In doing so, the volume of drilling work will be reduced by 2600 lin.m.

3. On the area of sheet M-42-VII, out of 8 projected boreholes (2800 lin.m), it is necessary to drill four (1400 lin.m) in 1983. Drilling of the remainder should be replaced by core documentation from boreholes of the Kustanai geological-geophysical expedition, which has projected drilling work here for 1983 accompanying geological survey at a scale of 1:50000.

4. On the area of sheet M-42-IV, 3 boreholes were planned (1050 lin.m). Drilling of these may be replaced by documentation from search boreholes of the Tselinograd GRE.

5. On the area of sheet M-42-V, it is necessary to drill three boreholes instead of the five projected, using for the study of Upper Paleozoic deposits the old borehole A, drilled by the Tselinograd GRE in the central part of the Aksanovsky syncline.

6. On sheet B-42-XXXV, the projected volume of drilling work should be retained, refining the placement of boreholes during the course of the work.

7. It is necessary to drill in 1983, 4 boreholes in the southeastern corner of sheet M-42-VII, where an absence of 1:50000 survey coverage of the North Kazakhstan and Central Kazakhstan production associations is observed.

8. Part of the borehole profile in the Shoshinkul syncline may be replaced by core documentation from reference-parametric borehole C20 (1050 lin.m), and in the Symaesk — by core documentation from search boreholes of the Turgai GRE.
