HEARD the report of sr. geologist, Mangystau Department CNIIS Smulyahinova E.M. on the results of work for 1979.

The party conducted survey-sample work on sheets M 1:200000 (territory sheets M-42-17-B, M-42-23 г., M-42-29 т., M-42-40; M-42-41).

The primary task of the Mangystau Department CNIIS for bulk mineral resources entailed conducting searches for deposits of uranium ore along the gamma-carving survey of search drilling.

The Teniz-Tenizi party conducted work in the southern part of the southern flank of the Tenizi depression, bounded to the north by anticline uplifts of Vedisprovskoye, Araldinskaya, Kairakschinskaya, Kainarsay, and supported by the Brezhnev shale. In the anticline structures, glauconite sands were encountered at depths of 350-470 m, and reversed relationships of abnormally high formation pressure were discovered.

The Commission reports:

I. During gamma-carotage in well 135 an abnormal gamma-activity surge of 49 micr/hour was detected against a background of 10-13 micr/hour at a depth of 457 m. The surge is associated with abnormal high formation pressure zones, with glauconite-bearing sandstones, enriched with dispersed bitumen. There is no anomalous practical significance; it is obvious, clearly associated with the surrounding rocks.

In wells 136, 140 at the transition of seroprotite formations typical activity in the redstone strata of the highest gamma-activity of 25-30 micr/hour against a background of 15-12 micr/hour, characteristic of this stratigraphic level, are associated with water-bearing limestones and sandstones, enriched with dispersed bitumen. These surges can be used for geological correlation of strata, i.e., they have clear stratigraphic significance in the southern and southwestern flank of the Tenizi depression.

The Commission establishes:

1. The objective of mass searches is completed.
2. Accept field materials on mass searches of the Zelenoe-Tenizi party for 1979 with the rating "Excellent"
