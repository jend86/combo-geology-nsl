THE CAMBRIAN SYSTEM (?)

Undifferentiated outcrops conventionally attributed to the
Cambrian system reach the surface during the day in a small
area ( - 0.5km ) block confined to the northwestern part of the
core of the Shabdara anticline, the rock outcrop is weak, the stratum
is represented by siliceous siltstones, mudstones, volcanomictic
and polymictic sandstones, yaimovidnye rocks, contains a layer of
acid tuffs composition, The color of the rocks is greenish- gray,
gray, brown, cherry-gray. The cutting capacity is 850m (Kolesnik,
1969). The lower boundary of the sediments is unknown; they are
overlain by sharply inconsistent conglomerates of the Dairin formation
and terrigenous deposits of the Middle-Upper Devonian.

isolate the described formations into an independent stratum, which
