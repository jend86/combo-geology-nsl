ITEMS FOR DISCUSSION: Report by Smolyaninova E.N. on practical results of aerogravimetric surveys in the West-Tien Shan region concerning the necessity of survey work and compilation of maps at scale 1:200000 for the southern half of the Tien Shan region.

1. In calculating the estimated budget for preparation of preliminary-oriented photoplans at scale 1:100000 in the West-Tien Shan region, the following volumes were utilized:
    area of work 22053 km²
    number of sheets at scale 1:100000 - 17 sheets, actual volumes:
    area of work 37720 km²
    number of sheets at scale 1:100000 - 20 sheets

Additionally, in the project, preparation of photoplans at various scales was not provided for, including at scales 1:2500 and 1:1700, which require compilation of intermediate photoplans at scales 1:2500 and 1:5000.

II. In the project Zapadno-Tengizskaya PSP on 6060 new cartographic and 27000 m of exploratory-cartographic drilling provided for sampling only 500 control samples (500 log.m).

Work experience showed that during drilling 4457 log.m of samples only 310 log.m of core samples (255 samples) and 454 geochemical samples were selected. The remaining selections were made on color differences of samples and on geochemical - without visible mineralization. During the initial sampling of geochemical varieties sampling was up to 2 m, and on color differences - up to 7 m.

The remaining volume of drilling requires 1600-1500 log.m of core sampling and 2500-2700 geochemical samples. Insufficient volume of core sampling amounts to 1200-1320 log.m.

Sampling of geochemical samples from core sections in the project plan was not provided. They will be selected 2700-3000 units at intervals of up to 5 m in color differences and up to 2 m in geochemical.

4. According to the instructions on compilation and preparation for publication of sheets of the state geological map of the USSR at scale I:200000 in areas of pole-exposed new paleozoic rocks one should consider the location of exposed paleozoic rocks, which in quality meet the requirements of productivity (or proximity to such).

In view of insufficient reserves or inadequate study degree in some areas, they should be assigned to a certain category of unreliability.

Proceeding from modern requirements of productivity and quality of measurements, on the map of pole paleozoic rocks it is necessary to show observations on content 0.2-0.3% at density 1.5 m or 0.3-0.45 m2.
