The paleontological description of the Tenisskaya suite fauna cannot be considered complete and exhaustive, since it covers only sections of the Tenisskaya suite. This is due to the absence in the authors' report of petrographic material from northern sections.

All Tenisskaya suite rocks in the studied section are represented by sandstones, siltstones, and argillic rocks, and also (in a subordinate quantity) by intracalceous gravel beds.
