**KAZFILIAL NRS RESOLVES:**

1. Accept the report of the West-Teniz Party on the geological re-study of the western part of the Teniz Depression at a scale of 1:200,000 with a rating of "Good."

The compiled geological maps shall be considered consistent with the scale of the work performed.

2. Disregard the figures proposed by the authors for the calculation of forecast resources of mineral deposits, due to methodologically incorrect execution of the calculation. The authors' recommendations shall be taken into account in the conduct of further investigations of the Teniz Depression.

3. Require the authors, prior to submission of the report to the archives:

1) To supplement the report with a geological assignment.

2) To indicate on the borehole columns the spatial reference of the latter (in the form of sheet nomenclature).

4. Recommend to PGO "Tsentrkazgeologiya":

1) To require the authors, in the conduct of further metallogenic work, to perform a calculation of forecast resources for the studied area in accordance with the requirements of the applicable instructions, and also to take into account the comments of the experts and the working commission of Kazfilial NRS regarding this report.

2) In the compilation of project proposals for geological-metallogenic work, to examine the question of the advisability of:

a) identifying new stratigraphic units (svity) taking into account the results of the survey and thematic investigations conducted;

b) compiling structural-metallogenic zoning maps in reports on geological survey work.

5. To draw the attention of the NTS of PGO "Tsentrkazgeologiya" to the necessity, in further comprehensive works on the Teniz Depression, of carrying out the correlation of geological boundaries on maps compiled in different years.
