RESOLUTION

OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE REGIONAL COUNCIL OF PEOPLE'S DEPUTIES

G. DZHAMBUL                    No. 129                    From 28 March 1979

"On conducting central geological-geophysical expeditions of the USSR on the territory of Arpalov and Don-Dzhilga districts"

HEARING the report on the conduct of geological-geochemical and borehole work, submitted by the Regional Council of People's Deputies,

RESOLVED:

In accordance with the Land Code of the Kazakh SSR articles 56, 57, authorize the Central Geological-Geophysical Expedition of the USSR to conduct geological-chemical and borehole work on the territory named after Nalbuyev, "Sostuchy" and Turgan agricultural-technical cooperatives of Arpakov district; named after Gogarin, named after Litov, named after Lenin of the Young Communist League, named after Lenin, named after Nekrasov, named after Surmanov, "Rostovskiy," "Dolindinskiy," "Berkulski" and corresponding consolidated Dzhambai district in the period from April to November 1979, under the conditions:

1. The central geological-geophysical expedition will conduct geological-chemical and borehole work on arable lands only until harvest and after completion of harvest of the crops.

2. According to the scope of geological-chemical and borehole work, those parcels where the upper soil layer will be disturbed, as necessary for use in agriculture, must be restored and soil must be returned to its original condition.

3. Report on the objects where geological-chemical and borehole work was conducted in 1978.

# LENINGRAD REGIONAL COUNCIL OF PEOPLE'S DEPUTIES
EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE
RESOLUTION

Leningrad
from 19.02.11

In-1 р

"On conducting geological survey work in the central geological survey search for oil and gas fields in agricultural land."

I. Postpone the central geological survey expedition conducted in 1981 geological-financial and prospecting work on the agricultural land of Alekseyevsky, Astrakhansky, Seletsky, Gribanovskiy, Krasnoznamenskiy, Kurganskiy, Mariinskiy, Tengushevskiy, Zelinogradskiy and Voronezhskiy districts in accordance with the established scheme of production work for all exploration drilling and based on data of agricultural land surveys.

1. Central geological survey expedition:
- Upon completion of survey work for agricultural crops, conduct field work after harvest, plowing on secondary roads and their general maintenance;
- Do not allow continuous passage of agricultural machinery through agricultural land, ensuring soil is preserved in places of work and relocation of survey equipment;
- During survey work, when digging wells and boreholes, provide strong upper fertile soil layer for subsequent reclamation of disturbed lands;
- Within established timeframes after completion of field work, transfer land plots to their owners in condition suitable for further agricultural use;
- Personally ensure the removal of all expedition equipment within the time period set for geological survey and exploratory work on the territories of administrative districts.

Chairperson of Executive Committee
[Signature]
Yu. Nozikov
