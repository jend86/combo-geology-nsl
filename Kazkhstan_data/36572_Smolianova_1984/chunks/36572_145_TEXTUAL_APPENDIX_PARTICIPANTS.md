On reconnaissance and survey work the following materials were obtained:

1. Cores of fantastical material with placement of pore-throat and capillary openings.
2. Geological sections on pore-throat openings - 9 m.
3. Samples documented of pore-throat openings-2 pcs.(56 sbs)
4. Stereoscopic diagrams on pore-throat openings - 9 pcs.
5. Stereoscopic diagrams on capillary openings - 55 pcs.
6. Diagrams of control relief-cartograms.
    The studied territory is located within the Franco-Russian arms, aerolian-terrigenous portions of middle carbon and neoproterozoic upper paleozoic.

Commission OPINION:

1. As a result of radiometric work, materials were obtained, permitting to clarify the dimensions according to geological structure. Radiometric anomalies in openings were not found.

2. In pore-throat opening 149 no complete relief-structure from several geological layers (saturation, zones and other openings in zone of interior pores). Interval relief-structure in opening 149 fully overlaps first pore-throat opening 150, in material 210-270m fine-grained qualitative relief-structure and radioactive openings not detected.

3. Control methods sufficient, quality of material good.

Recommendations and conclusions:

1.Regularly examine journal of relief-structure.

Commission statement:

1. Properly and expeditiously complete volume of drilling work.
2. Inform materials Zemlya-Genezskoe USSR right of opinion "good".
