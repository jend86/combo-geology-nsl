Above lies a sequence of bright-red rocks, which by their lithological composition occupies an intermediate position between the deposits of the Tersakkanskoye and Kireyskaya formations.

The thickness of this sequence is 57m. It is practically impossible to identify the formation from it. We assign this sequence of rocks in the given specific section to the Kireyskaya Formation. Apparently, where it has a coarser-grained composition, it can be attributed to the Tersakkanskoye Formation. Areally, apparently, in this interval there is a sliding of the boundary.
