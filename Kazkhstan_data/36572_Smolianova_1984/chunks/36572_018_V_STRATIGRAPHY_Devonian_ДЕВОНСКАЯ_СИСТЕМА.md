The accumulation of volcanogenic and volcanogenic-terrigenous
deposits of the Early-Middle Devonian age occurred, as noted by
almost all researchers who studied and mapped the described
strata, in the margins of relatively small intermountain
depressions and is genetically closely related to the zones of
long-lived regional faults. The prirazlomny character of the Shizhno-
Srednedevonsky effusive magmatism of the studied territory is especially

the described strata are confined to the sublatitudinal zone along

occurs and practically does not extend either north or west of
this strip. This structural position is also characteristic of other areas
of the distribution of the described formations within the area of work
(Kireysky ledge; Ishim Bow), although the connection with discontinuous
disturbances is less clearly manifested here.
