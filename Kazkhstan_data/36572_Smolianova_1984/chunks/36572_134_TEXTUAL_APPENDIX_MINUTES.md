**HEARD:** Report by senior geologist of the West-Teniz Party, Naumenko V.V., on the results of mass prospecting work over the work area.

The target objective of the party for mass prospecting in 1982 was the search for uranium deposits by means of gamma-logging of boreholes within the map sheet areas М-42–6,8,18; В-42–138–140.

The following materials were submitted for commission review:

1. Maps of factual data showing the placement of prospecting-mapping and mapping boreholes.

2. Borehole documentation journals.

3. Geological core logs for boreholes.

4. Logging diagrams for boreholes.

5. Control gamma-logging diagrams.

The investigated territory is composed of Ordovician, Devonian, Carboniferous, and variegated upper Paleozoic deposits.

As a result of the work carried out, a radiometric characterization of the rocks in the area was obtained, and during gamma-logging one anomaly was identified in borehole No. 169, associated with the grey sandstones of the Nizhneturneian substage.

Maximum gamma activity of 152 to 186 μR/hr was recorded in the interval 244.0–249.0 m against a background of 25 μR/hr.

According to core sample analysis from the above-mentioned interval, the uranium content in them ranges from 0.001 to 0.003% and thorium up to 0.001%, while in one sample uranium is 0.009%, radium 0.01% and thorium less than 0.001%. Companion elements: copper up to 0.008%, zinc up to 0.002%, lead up to 0.002%, molybdenum up to 0.00015% and phosphorus from 0.4 to 0.6%.

The anomaly in borehole No. 169 has unclear prospects and represents exploration interest.

It should be noted that it is located within the Dabai prospective area, recommended by a specialized organization for deep exploration drilling.

**The Commission NOTES:**

1. The mass prospecting was conducted in accordance with the project and is methodologically correct.

2. As a result of the radiometric work, materials were obtained that allow subdivision of the section by lithological composition.

3. The control gamma-logging was performed in sufficient volume with good reproducibility.

**The Commission RESOLVES:**

To accept the field materials from the mass prospecting of the Zapadno-Tenizskaya party for the year 1962 with a rating of **"good"**.
