CONCLUSION

On stage 01 "Preliminary prospecting work" for geological exploration of the Ustyurt-Mangystau section, approved by chief engineer dated November 3, 1977

Commission consisting of:

ANTONKA R.M.          - commission chairman, senior geologist of NTGO
BURMAN V.I.           - chief geologist
SMOLYANINOVA G.N.     - head of geological department
MISHCHENKO E.A.       - senior specialist of PGO
GULNEVA A.A.          - representative of the Geological Prospect NTGO

Having reviewed the results of the proposed prospecting work and having obtained information (project, estimate, geological maps 1:100000, materials of previous studies and core research), accepts the completed stage of work with the rating "satisfactory" for submission to the report for corresponding factually completed volume in the sum of 26944 rubles.

Quality assessment and sum of work is determined based on the following criteria:

1. Geological task for the stage completed.
2. All submitted materials both in volume and quality correspond to the established task.
3. Deviation from the accepted methodology absent.
4. Coordination of complex work according to stage plan.

U. 1101 cc

-315-

5. Deadline for completion of work:
beginning - January 1978, fact - January 1978
completion - May 1979, fact - April 1979

6. Obtained main results
a) Compiled detailed photogeological maps of the area corresponding to contemporary requirements.
b) On the area of geological study, a catalog of geological-geophysical and scientific information was compiled.

Commission recommends:

1. Before sending the work to the field, separate cosmic aerophotographs.
2. Submit on geological maps geophysical anomalies and disturbances of seismic profiles.
3. On geological maps apply passage media and attempt to identify regularities in their distribution.
