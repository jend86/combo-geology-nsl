**DECISION**
**of the Executive Committee of the Regional Council of People's Deputies**

**City of Arkalyk** **No. 6/129** **dated March 28, 1979**

*"On the Conduct by the Central Geological-Geophysical Expedition of TsKTGU of Geological Survey Works on the Territory of Farms in the Arkalyk and Dzhangalinsky Districts"*

---

Having reviewed the materials concerning the conduct of geological survey and drilling works, the Executive Committee of the Regional Council of People's Deputies,

**DECIDES:**

In accordance with Articles 56 and 57 of the Land Code of the Kazakh SSR, to permit the Central Geological-Geophysical Expedition of TsKTGU to conduct geological survey and drilling works on the territory of the following farms: named after Maikutov, "Vostochny," and the Turgai State Farm Technical School of the Arkalyk District; named after Gagarin, named after Titov, named after the XXI Congress of the CPSU, named after the Leninist Komsomol, named after Nakhimov, named after Furmanov, "Rostovsky," "Dzhanadalinsky," "Verenkulshy," and the district specialized farm association of the Dzhanadalinsky District, during the period from April through November of 1979, under the following conditions:

1. The Central Geological-Geophysical Expedition shall conduct geological survey and drilling works on arable lands only before the sowing season and after the completion of the harvest.

2. Upon completion of geological survey and drilling works, land plots where the upper fertile layer has been disturbed shall be restored to a condition suitable for agricultural use and handed over to the district commissions by act.

3. To submit acts for all sites where geological survey and drilling works were carried out in 1978.

City of Tselinograd

"On the conduct of geological exploration work by the Central Geological Prospecting Expedition on the land-use territory of the region."

I. To permit the Central Geological Prospecting Expedition to conduct geophysical and exploration works in 1981 on the land holdings of Alekseyevsky, Astrakhansky, Seletinsky, Yermantausky, Krasnoznamenskiy, Kurgaldzhinsky, Marinovskiy, Tengizsky, Tselinogradsky, and Shortandinsky districts in accordance with the proposed work production scheme, without the right to excavate trenches and ditches on the said agricultural lands.

I. The Central Geological Prospecting Expedition:

— in order to avoid destruction of agricultural crops, to conduct prospecting works after the harvest has been collected, and drilling works along field roads and their shoulders;

— to prevent unsystematic movement of machinery across land-use territories, contamination of land at machinery work sites and at geologists' camp locations;

— during drilling operations, excavation of ditches and trenches, to ensure removal of the upper fertile soil layer for subsequent recultivation of disturbed lands;

— within one week after completion of works, to transfer the used lands to the farms as part of their previous holdings, in a condition suitable for further use in agriculture;

— personal responsibility for the proper use of lands provided to the expedition to be placed upon the chiefs of geological parties and chief engineers of [illegible] districts.
