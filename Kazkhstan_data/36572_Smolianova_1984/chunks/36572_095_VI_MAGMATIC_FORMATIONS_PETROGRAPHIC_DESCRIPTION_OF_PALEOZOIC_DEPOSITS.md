The most promising according to paleontological and stratigraphic grounds section of this region belongs to the Middle Paleozoic interval, which was described by the authors in the journal Priroda in the Kokur river valley (7666/5).

The described section is located on the left bank of the river, at 2.5 km from the Belogorsk settlement (N-42-XXXIV). General considerations allow us to hope that this will make it possible to fill the gaps in our knowledge of regional paleontological data.
