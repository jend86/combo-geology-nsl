4. The complex of works provided for by the phased plan has been utilized.

5. Timeline for completion of the work phase.

Start — May 1979, actual — May 1979.
Completion — March 1980, actual — March 1980.

6. The following principal results were obtained:

a) The compiled field geological maps at a scale of 1:200,000 differ from previously published ones in considerably greater detail, both in the stratigraphic subdivision of the Upper Paleozoic sequences and in the delineation of structures.

b) In the study of Upper Paleozoic sequences, greater attention was paid to the search for oil-bearing sandstones at the Archalinskiy and Elanskiy stratigraphic levels.

Detailed study was made of partial cross-sections of the lithological composition of the sequences and the character of facies variation in space and time.

7. Comments on the available materials:

a) Young faults, expressed in and manifested through the geomorphological appearance of the region, were not reflected on the geological maps.

b) No analysis was made of the distribution of ore occurrences on a new structural basis.

8. Recommendations:

a) To compile isopach and facies diagrams for individual suites, bundles, and sequences.

b) Using geophysical materials, to carry out an analysis of the dependence of the structural pattern of the Upper Paleozoic cover of the depression upon its basement.
