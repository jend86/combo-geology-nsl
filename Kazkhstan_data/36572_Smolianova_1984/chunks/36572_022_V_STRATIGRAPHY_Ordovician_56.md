The claystones of the Kalmykian series contain magnetite, with magnetic properties of 100.10⁻⁶ units.cgs. In the magnetic field above the magnetic roof are laid out spotty-type pods.

The average density of claystones ranges from 2.64 g/cm³ in aleurolites up to 2.69 g/cm³ in silt stones; medium density of series 2.66 g/cm³, differing in medium density of argillite. In the granulometric field specific gravity is not manifested.
