2. Methodology of work.

Core-sample search and prospecting specimens were performed by PGS UPPO (operator Noserov D.), North-Western core hole section UPPO (operator Sidukhin N.) and Kolmekskoy core hole section UPPO (operator Upyshenko A.N.). Work was performed in accordance with instructions for gamma-carotage (1976).

Gamma-ray logs of core-sample sections were produced using PPC-2 with automatic recorder on a diagram tape at speeds of 1200. Before beginning core logging, apparatus verification was performed on standard samples. Gamma-ray activity details were recorded at scales 1:50 and logging speed was not more than 50 m/hour. Log recording speed was 250m/hour. This calibration of the carotage radiometer PPC-2 with reduction C-41 was performed twice a month and a month later. Sensitivity verification was performed before and after each work series from the working station. Results of these checks are recorded on a diagram tape and in a journal.
